About PCNL


Philippine College of Northwestern Luzon (PCNL) is a private, non-sectarian college in Agoo, La Union. It offers four-year undergraduate programs and also technical-vocational programs that can be completed in less than a year. The bachelor's degree programs available are in Elementary and Secondary Education, Information Technology, Criminology, Accountancy, and Business Administration. The TVET programs offered by TESDA are Computer Hardware Servicing, Housekeeping, and Programming, all leading to a National Certificate (NC).



Our Values


MISSION:In support of its philosophy, PCNL mandates itself to provide advanced quality instruction to its clientele in education, the arts and humanities, the sciences, technology, business and other related professional and technical fields; promote the advancement of knowledge through research; and help improve the quality of community life through extension work.


VISION:To be a distinctive learning institution acknowledge far and wide for its innovative and flexible approaches in working with its students, stakeholders and the community to create a more just and human society and more sustainable economy.
